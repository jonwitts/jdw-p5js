A chain of linked pendulums. Each a little shorter and faster than the one it's linked to.

Each joint of the pendulum leaves behind its own trail.

Keys 1: toggle pendulum, 2: toggle pendulum path, -: decrease speed relation, +: increase speed relation, arrow down: decrease length of lines, arrow up: increase length of lines, arrow left: decrease joints, arrow right: increase joints, del or backspace: clear screen, s: save png
